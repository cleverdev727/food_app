Troubleshooting guide The oven won't turn on.
  • Oven needs to be in flipped-down position in order to power it on.
  • Ensure the power cord is securely plugged into the outlet.
  • Ensure the power cord is not damaged.
  • Insert the power cord into a different outlet in
    a different room that is on a different circuit.
  • Press the power button.
The oven is generating smoke.
  • Run a toast cycle to burn off excess grease.
  • Cleaned the oven
The heating element is not lighting up.
  • This is normal. Different function is adjusting the heating
    elements'power levels, which set heating elements turning
    on and off at different times. Sometimes heating elements
    will not visibly glow.
  • To be sure, you could turn off product and try on another
    program (e.g.air fry) to recheck element.
There is noise coming from the control panel.
  • This is normal. Some functions are with fan; some functions
    are not. When fan working, product sounds will be bigger.
The coating on the accessories chipped or worn.
  • The coating helps provide easier cleanup after cooking.
  • Aerosol sprays, abrasive cleaners, sharp utensils, and
    brushed metalutensils are not recommended.
  • Placing accessories in the dishwasher may cause wearing of
    the coating and rust on the metal rails.
  • Hand-washing in warm, soapy water is highly recommended
    for cleaning.
Why is steam coming out from the oven door?
  • This is normal. Foods with a high moisture content may
    release steam around the door.
Why is water dripping onto the desk the oven door?
  • This is normal. Foods with a high moisture content (such as
    frozen breads) may release inside the oven and drip onto the desk
