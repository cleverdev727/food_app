Menu code: 001 Grilled Corn with Butter
10-20 min
35째C
Ingredients 50g milk
20g unsalted Butter
5g white sugar
10g condensed milk
Step 1 Put 15 grams of butter in a bowl, pour 50 grams of milk, 10 grams
of white sugar, use a Weibo stove to bite the butter to melt, stir well;
Step 2 Take it out and prepare to eat (be careful of burning your mouth).
Step 3 Brush the corn, preheat the oven at 200 degrees, and bake for 10
minutes (note that the corn is cooked, because it can retain the
moisture and nutrients of the corn to the maximum), and the corn
can be turned over during the period.
